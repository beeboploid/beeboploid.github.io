I MUST HAVE A PHILOSOPHY
I believe that true motivation lies in fear. The most effective kind of motivation-inducing anxiety springs from the social contracts we form with others and the assurances we give ourselves when something must be done. This is a task-managing app that will tap into my primordial fear of dissappointing not only myself but those around me. If it doesn't meet this goal, I fear I will have wasted several hours preparing this proposal.
I MUST MAKE COMMITMENTS

In this app, a 'task' is referred to as a 'commitment.' Each commitment is made 'To' someone, and 'For' a particular task, and has a deadline.
